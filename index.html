Why EllaVe

EllaVe is a natural soap without any chemicals

All natural soap is made with essential oils, while regular soap is made with harsh chemicals. Natural soap doesn't contain any harsh chemicals that can dry out or irritate your skin. In fact, natural soap is often packed with nourishing ingredients like shea butter and vitamin E that actually benefit your skin!

Organic soaps are healthier for your body. The natural products and ingredients that are sourced and used in a handmade soap bar are what our body and skin are trained to expect. They also cleanse and moisturize our skin naturally. This is in stark contrast to the bar or liquid soap that is mass-produced.
